Our Story

Kind Heart Charity, Non-Profit Organization

Joyland organization center is not a profit organization helping to break the cycle of poverty by proving educational opportunities to orphans and underprivileged children. Is located in Esilalei Ward, Mto wa mbu Arusha Tanzania.
Joyland organization aims to provide schooling, healthcare and other essential needs to orphans and vulnerable children in Tanzania. The center provide a home for 24 orphans. It also privides nursery education to local children whose families are unable to send them to government school.
Joyland Organizartion started on 2018, the founders are Gasper John Ngalawa and Ruth Wilson Mtatuu. The reason for this Organization is due to lack of quality/ best education to poor families (the less privileged and orphans).
Joyland provide also home for 24 children. Provide nursery school education through english as a medium of communiction.
So Joyland is the hope to the children from poor families to get best education.
